Talking to Friends and Family

What is Email?

Email is a fast way to send digital letters to your friends and family. Your "Inbox" is where you find mail you have received, while "Compose" is the button you use to write a new message.

Video Calls

Video calls let you see and hear your family through your computer screen in real-time. Make sure your volume is turned up and sit in a well-lit room so your loved ones can see your smile clearly.

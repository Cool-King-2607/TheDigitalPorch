Finding Your Way Online

The Address Bar: Your Digital Map

The bar at the very top of your screen is like the address line on an envelope. When you know exactly where you want to go, type the name there and press the "Enter" key on your keyboard.

The Search Bar: Asking a Question

If you aren't sure of the address, you can type a question into the Search Bar just like you are talking to a librarian. For example, you can type "what is the weather today" to see a list of helpful answers.

The Magic "Back" Button

The arrow pointing to the left is your "undo" button and is the most important tool to remember. If you ever feel lost or click something by mistake, simply click this arrow to go back to safety.

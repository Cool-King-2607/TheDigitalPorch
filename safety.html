Safety

How to Spot a "Fake" Message

If a message asks for money or tries to scare you, it is likely a trick called a scam. Real banks will never ask for your private passwords in an email, so it is always safe to simply delete those messages.

Safely Shopping Online

Shopping online is safe as long as you look for the "Closed Padlock" icon at the very top of your screen. This little symbol means the website is locked and your personal information is kept private.

Helpful Words

A "Link" is a blue or underlined word that takes you to a new page when you click it. "WiFi" is the invisible signal that connects your computer to the rest of the world so you can browse the web. A "Tab" is basically a bookmark that lets you keep multiple pages open at the same time
